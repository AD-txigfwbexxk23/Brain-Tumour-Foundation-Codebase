FAQ

Is this tool a medical device?

No. It is a decision support tool and does not replace clinical judgment.

Where does inference run?

All inference runs locally on the server hosting this application; no data leaves your environment.

What inputs are required?

Age, Tumor Size, Growth Rate, Symptom Severity, Tumor Location, MRI Findings, and Radiation Exposure.
